##  Applied Projects: The Kenyan Agrifood Value Chain

Instead of generic datasets, my portfolio consists of six targeted projects that tell the story of the agricultural lifecycle. These span across Spreadsheets, SQL, and Power BI.

###  Spreadsheet Analytics
**1. ASALtd Contract Farming Dashboard (Excel)** 
* **Phase:** *Production (In the Soil)*
* **Description:** An advanced Excel dashboard built for a fictional contract farming company (ASALtd) operating in Kenya's former Eastern region. The system tracks the entire contract lifecycle for cereal farmers, bridging data across farmer registries, active contracts, harvest deliveries, payment disbursements, and final sales. 

**2. NGO Input & Soil Health Tracker (Google Sheets)**
* **Phase:** *Pre-Planting (Before the Soil)*
* **Description:** A cloud-based Google Sheets reporting tool designed for field agents to replace paper-based tracking. It analyzes the distribution of drought-resistant seeds and fertilizers against localized soil testing data to optimize input allocations for smallholders.

###  Relational Databases (SQL)
**3. Agrifood Market & Inventory Relational Database**
* **Phase:** *The Market*
* **Description:** A normalized SQL database designed to track fluctuating urban market prices against rural inventory levels. Utilizes complex joins and window functions to query real-time supply and demand, helping SMEs optimize their purchasing strategies.

**4. Post-Harvest Logistics & Spoilage Tracker**
* **Phase:** *Transit (After the Soil)*
* **Description:** An optimized database querying transport routes, cold-chain integrity, and delivery timelines. By leveraging advanced SQL analytical functions, this project identifies exactly where in the supply chain perishable goods are being delayed and lost.

###  Business Intelligence (Power BI)
**5. National Food Waste & Recycling Loop Dashboard**
* **Phase:** *Waste & Recycle*
* **Description:** An interactive Power BI report mapping urban food waste volumes against agricultural recycling initiatives (like black soldier fly larvae composting). Uses Power Query to clean disparate municipal data and DAX to calculate the volume of waste successfully converted back into animal feed.

**6. Agribusiness Sales & End-to-End Reporting Dashboard**
* **Phase:** *The Big Picture*
* **Description:** A capstone Power BI dashboard integrating data from production, logistics, and market sales. It transforms complex, multi-departmental datasets into an accessible visual story, empowering agribusiness executives to make data-driven, strategic decisions at a glance.
