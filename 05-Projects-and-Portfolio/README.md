#  Applied Analytics Portfolio: The Kenyan Agrifood Value Chain

This directory contains the six capstone projects of my Data Analytics portfolio. Rather than analyzing disparate, generic datasets, these projects are designed to tell a cohesive, end-to-end story of the Kenyan agricultural lifecycle. 

By utilizing Spreadsheets, SQL, and Power BI, I have mapped data solutions to specific phases of the food system—from pre-planting soil preparation to post-consumer recycling.

###  Spreadsheet Analytics
*   **[01. ASALtd Contract Farming Dashboard](./01-ASALtd-Contract-Farming-Dashboard/)** (Excel)
    *   **Phase:** Production (In the Soil)
    *   **Focus:** Advanced formulas, PivotTables, and dashboarding to track farmer contracts, cereal deliveries, and payments in Kenya's former Eastern region.
*   **[02. NGO Input & Soil Health Tracker](./02-NGO-Input-Soil-Tracker/)** (Google Sheets)
    *   **Phase:** Pre-Planting (Before the Soil)
    *   **Focus:** Cloud-based data validation and automated reporting replacing manual field-agent tracking for drought-resistant seed distribution.

###  Relational Databases (SQL)
*   **[03. Agrifood Market & Inventory Relational Database](./03-Agrifood-Market-Inventory-DB/)** (SQL)
    *   **Phase:** The Market
    *   **Focus:** Database normalization, complex joins, and window functions analyzing real-time urban demand against rural supply.
*   **[04. Post-Harvest Logistics & Spoilage Tracker](./04-Post-Harvest-Logistics-Tracker/)** (SQL)
    *   **Phase:** Transit (After the Soil)
    *   **Focus:** Querying transport routes and cold-chain integrity to identify exact bottlenecks causing perishable food loss.

###  Business Intelligence (Power BI)
*   **[05. National Food Waste & Recycling Loop Dashboard](./05-Food-Waste-Recycling-Loop/)** (Power BI)
    *   **Phase:** Waste & Recycle
    *   **Focus:** Power Query transformations and DAX mapping urban food waste volumes against black soldier fly larvae composting initiatives.
*   **[06. Agribusiness Sales & End-to-End Reporting Dashboard](./06-Agribusiness-Sales-Dashboard/)** (Power BI)
    *   **Phase:** The Big Picture
    *   **Focus:** A comprehensive executive dashboard integrating production, logistics, and sales data to drive strategic, data-led decisions for SMEs.
